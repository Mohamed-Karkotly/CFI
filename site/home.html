About Us

We work with top brands

City Lights Fragrances International LTD, established in 1995 with its first perfume retail outlet in Aleppo, Syria. With ambitious growth plan by dedicated and creative leadership, throughout these years, we have substantially grown in size by expanding its wholesale outlet in Aleppo and other provinces in Syria, and outside Syria.
Today, we are expanding our perfume outlets in major new generation shops, so we moved our base location into Turkey and kept our buisness in Syria, also preserved fulfilling a mantra of excellence in the industry with very efficient services.
We are dedicated to work together with uncompromising ethics and integrity. We encourage our people to create, innovate, and to strive for the best always.
We aim to be a high-end innovative and creative fragrance organisation that caters to all categories of fragrance creation and develops a working partnership with our clients for a successful and everlasting business relationship.
As we pursue our journey, we are investing higher resources in our most promising opportunities – by category, brand and region.
We carefully decide where to allocate our resources to drive our momentum for the long term.
Customers of , appreciate the service-orientated attitude and the unique shopping experience with offering the highest quality and very latest in all categories of product ranges including exclusive offers and gift ranges.
At , we believe your shopping experience should be effortless, enjoyable and worth it!
