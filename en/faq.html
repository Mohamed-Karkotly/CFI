Here are some of the most commonly asked questions about our business and our products.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. But please refer that your order may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are levied once a package reaches your country.
We have no control over these charges and cannot predict what the costs may be.
Customs policies vary from country to country; you should contact your local customs office for further information.

Where can I find pricing information for your fragrance oils and other products?

The pricing information for the fragrance oils and other products can be sent directly to your e-mail, just fill out the information Here.
Pricing on fragrances will vary depending on the fragrance and the size bottle you order.

Why do your fragrance oil prices vary?

The prices of the fragrances are based on the materials used to create them. Some of the raw materials are more expensive than others, so that is reflected in the price of the individual fragrance.
The fragrances that are more expensive cost more to produce.

Are your fragrances essential oils?

While some of our fragrance oils contain natural ingredients, the fragrances that we carry are classified as synthetic or man made.
Essential oils are extracted from plant material and, for that reason, are usually much more expensive.

Does your Fine Fragrance collection smell the same as the originals?

Yes. All the perfume oils for our generic perfume range are imported from Europe and are of the highest quality. In addition each of our generics goes through a rigorous in house “Nose” test to check that the smell is a perfect match to the original, before it is introduced into our range. Yes we compare it against the original to ensure that the smell is a perfect match!

How long does it take for my order to be delivered?

Deliveries can take up to 5-7 working days after the funds reflect in our account.
P.S. It can take up to 48 hours from the time you do the transfer for the funds to reflect in our account.

Can I smell the “Inspired by” fine fragrance before I buy?

Yes. You can visit any of our stores to try before you buy.

How can you offer such high quality “Inspired by” fine fragrances at such affordable prices?

The answer is simple. Like the manufacturers of ‘generic’ products, all our “Inspired by” fine fragrances have the qualities and effectiveness of the originals but without us having to spend masses of money on branding, licensing, marketing, advertising, sponsorships, etc. etc.
This means we can pass these cost savings onto you the consumer.

Do you offer samples?

We offer samples to existing customers and prospective clients.
We receive many requests per week of prospective clients wanting samples, But we have to make sure that we prescreen our customers to evaluate their needs.

What is your minimum order??

We have a 5 KG minimum per fragrance on all orders.
We don’t want to go lower than 5 KG because this becomes too labor intensive to keep our lower price.
The labor to make 5 KG to 500 KG is not that much different. The difference of 500 KG is at least 10% off the selling price compared to 5 KG of the same fragrance.
